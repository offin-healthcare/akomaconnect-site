## DETAILED BROCHURE

### The problem
Hospitals across the continent run on a patchwork of paper, spreadsheets and disconnected
software — and most systems assume reliable power and internet that simply isn't there.
The result: lost records, duplicated work, billing leakage, and compliance exposure.

### The solution
AkomaConnect unifies the whole facility on one platform with a single, trusted patient
record — engineered for real African operating conditions and for the regulations you
actually answer to.

### Capabilities at a glance

**Clinical** — OPD & encounters, diagnoses & clinical pathways, maternity/ANC, pharmacy
(five-rights dispensing, controlled-drug dual verification, FEFO), rehabilitation
(outcome measures, instrumented gait/EMG), community health (CHW, immunisation), dental,
physiotherapy, dietary, mortuary.

**Ancillary** — laboratory (orders, QC, critical-value alerts), radiology, blood bank
(cross-match, two-person transfusion verification, cold-chain), CSSD, supply chain
(procure-to-stock, three-way match, FEFO).

**Patient care** — master patient index with de-duplication, live patient-journey board,
queue & waiting-room display with voice call-out, inpatient ADT with live ward map and
occupancy forecasting, day case, emergency, referrals.

**Back-office** — billing & payments (mobile money + cards, tamper-proof receipts),
double-entry finance & GL, country-specific HR & payroll, and a financial-integration
gateway that posts to your accounting system reliably and asynchronously.

**Add-ons** — AI & clinical decision support, telemedicine, patient portal (SMS login),
call centre, analytics & MIS dashboards, compliance center, and device gateways.

### Built differently
- **Offline-first PWA:** installable, caches the workspace, queues writes, syncs on reconnect.
- **Simple, robust core:** PostgreSQL is the only stateful dependency — fewer moving parts, easier to run and recover.
- **Interoperable:** FHIR R4 API, HL7/MLLP device feeds, accounting connectors.
- **Secure & accountable:** MFA, encrypted patient identifiers (PII), complete audit log, automated backups and a documented disaster-recovery runbook.
- **Responsible AI:** advisory only, confidence-scored, human-in-the-loop, override-logged, fairness-monitored.

### Compliance
Jurisdiction-driven Compliance Center. In Ghana: HeFRA facility licensing and the Data
Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) — consent management, data-subject requests (access,
portability, erasure, rectification), retention policies, and breach notification within
the statutory window. Country profiles ship for Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa.

### Deployment & support
Cloud, on-premise, or hybrid from a single container image. Backups, monitoring and a
disaster-recovery plan are built in. Implementation, data migration from your existing
records, training and ongoing support are provided by Offin Healthcare.
