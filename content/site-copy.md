## Value propositions (the "why")

### 1. Works offline. Syncs when you're back online.
Power cuts and patchy connectivity shouldn't stop care. AkomaConnect runs as an
installable app that caches your workspace and reference data, queues what you enter,
and syncs automatically the moment the connection returns. Clinicians keep working;
nothing is lost.

### 2. Compliant by design — for your jurisdiction.
Compliance isn't bolted on. AkomaConnect is jurisdiction-aware: select your country and
the platform applies the right regime — in Ghana, **HeFRA** licensing plus the **Data
Protection Act (Act 843)** with its 72-hour breach clock and data-subject rights.
Profiles ship for Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa.

### 3. One system, clinical to back-office.
Stop stitching together point solutions. Encounters, labs, pharmacy, wards, billing,
finance, HR and analytics live in one coherent platform with a single patient record —
so data flows instead of being re-keyed.

### 4. Interoperable and open.
A built-in **HL7 FHIR R4** API, **HL7/MLLP device gateways** (ECG, vitals, lab
analysers) and direct **accounting integrations** (QuickBooks, Sage, Tally, Odoo and
more) mean AkomaConnect fits the systems you already run.

### 5. Reliable and secure.
A deliberately simple architecture — PostgreSQL as the only stateful dependency — means
fewer moving parts and easier operations. Multi-factor authentication, encrypted patient
identifiers, full audit trails, automated backups and a documented disaster-recovery
plan protect your data.

### 6. Safe, transparent AI — advice, never autopilot.
Optional clinical decision support (sepsis, maternal risk, triage) **advises only**,
always shows a confidence score, keeps a human in the loop, logs every override, and is
continuously monitored for fairness across patient groups.

## Modules

AkomaConnect is modular. Facilities switch on what they need; capabilities scale by tier.

### Clinical
- **OPD & Encounters** — structured consultations, diagnoses (ICD-10), and clinical pathways.
- **Maternity & ANC** — antenatal visits, EDD and risk scoring, PMTCT prompts.
- **Pharmacy** — dispensing with the five rights, controlled-drug dual verification, FEFO stock, refills.
- **Rehabilitation** — episodes, standardised outcome measures, instrumented gait & EMG analysis.
- **Community Health (CHW)** — household registers, outreach visits, immunisation schedules, offline sync.
- **Dental, Physiotherapy, Dietary, Mortuary** — specialty workflows where you need them.

### Ancillary
- **Laboratory (LIS)** — orders, accessioning, results, QC, critical-value alerts.
- **Radiology (RIS)** — orders, studies, structured reporting.
- **Blood Bank** — donor screening, cross-match, two-person transfusion verification, cold-chain.
- **CSSD & Supply Chain** — sterilisation cycles; procure-to-stock with FEFO and three-way match.

### Patient Care
- **Master Patient Index** — one trusted record, fuzzy de-duplication, merge/unmerge.
- **Patient Journey & Queue** — live board, waiting-room display with voice call-out.
- **Inpatient (ADT)** — admissions, transfers, discharges, live ward map, occupancy forecast.
- **Day Case, Emergency, Referrals** — the full pathway through the facility.

### Back-Office
- **Billing & Payments** — invoicing, cashier sessions, mobile money (M-Pesa) and cards; receipts are never reversed by a sync failure.
- **Finance & General Ledger** — double-entry GL, budgets, fixed assets, statements.
- **HR & Payroll** — country-specific statutory payroll (SSNIT/PAYE, NSSF/SHIF, and more), leave, credentials.
- **Financial Integration Gateway** — posts financial events to your accounting system, reliably and asynchronously.

### Add-Ons
- **AI & Clinical Decision Support** · **Telemedicine** · **Patient Portal (SMS login)** ·
  **Call Centre** · **Analytics & MIS dashboards** · **Compliance Center** ·
  **Device Gateways (VitaLink)** · and more.

## Deployment — your data, your choice

The same software runs three ways, so you're never locked in:

- **Cloud** — we host and maintain it; you sign in from anywhere. Fastest to start.
- **On-premise** — runs on a server inside your facility for full data residency and
  offline resilience. Ideal where connectivity is unreliable or sovereignty is required.
- **Hybrid** — a central cloud for the group with on-premise nodes at each site.

One container image, one operational model, deployed wherever you need it.
