Why facilities choose AkomaConnect

⚡

Works offline

Power cuts and patchy connectivity won't stop care. The app caches your workspace, queues what you enter, and syncs automatically when you're back online.

🛡️

Compliant by design

Jurisdiction-aware: HeFRA licensing and Ghana's Data Protection Act (Act 843) with its 72-hour breach clock. Profiles for GH, KE, NG, ZA.

🧩

One system

Clinical, ancillary, patient flow, billing, finance, HR and analytics on a single patient record — data flows instead of being re-keyed.

🔌

Interoperable

Built-in HL7 FHIR R4, HL7/MLLP device gateways (ECG, vitals, analysers) and accounting integrations (QuickBooks, Sage, Tally, Odoo).

🔒

Reliable & secure

A simple PostgreSQL core, MFA, encrypted patient identifiers, full audit trails, automated backups and a documented DR plan.

🧠

Safe, transparent AI

Decision support that advises only — always confidence-scored, human-in-the-loop, override-logged and fairness-monitored.
